Internships & Arts Immersions

Long-form creative immersion programs across Indonesia.

This is not a short retreat. It is not a course. And it is not a tour.

These internships are long-form immersions for students and emerging creatives who want to live inside artistic and cultural practice—not observe it from a distance.

Duration

  • 1 Month — Introductory immersion
  • 4 Months — Core apprenticeship (recommended)
  • 6 Months — Deep integration

Longer stays allow the experience to move from observation → embodiment → contribution.

Locations & Focus

Bali

Visual arts • ritual craft • interdisciplinary practice

A culture where art, ritual, and daily life are inseparable. Ideal for artists drawn to symbolic depth, repetition, and studio-based practice.

Lombok

Craft traditions • material practice • simplicity

Grounded, slow, and elemental. Best for those seeking humility, hand-making, and material intelligence.

Yogyakarta

Contemporary art • experimentation • cultural dialogue

Indonesia’s creative heart—where tradition and innovation meet. Ideal for exploratory and expressive practitioners.

Jakarta

Urban arts • media • design • cultural production

Dynamic and outward-facing. For participants interested in professional creative ecosystems and modern systems.

Visa & Accommodation

  • Visa sponsorship and coordination
  • Accommodation options (shared, private, or homestay)
  • Orientation and ongoing local support

Participants typically cover flights, personal expenses, and materials related to their practice.

Who This Is For

  • Fine arts and design students
  • Writers, filmmakers, and cultural practitioners
  • Gap-year creatives
  • Emerging facilitators and educators

This program is not designed for those seeking certificates, fast outcomes, or rigid schedules.

Relationship to Archetype Retreats

Archetype Retreats offers short-form catalytic experiences. The Internship Pathway exists for those who want to stay longer— allowing insight to become practice, and practice to become a way of living.

Apply

Placements are limited and application-based to preserve depth and integrity.
