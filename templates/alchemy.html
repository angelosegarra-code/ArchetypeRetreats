What Is Alchemy?

Alchemy is a structured group experience where insight emerges through interaction, not instruction. Participants are guided through archetypal prompts and reflections that reveal how patterns protect — and how they may quietly limit.

  • Guided reflection and archetypal prompts
  • Group mirroring (with consent, without judgment)
  • No diagnosing, no fixing, no labels
  • A clear structure held by a facilitator

What Happens in a Session

A small group gathers. Cards are drawn. Questions are answered. Participants reflect — not only on themselves, but on how others experience them. Patterns begin to appear, not because they are explained, but because they are seen.

1

Arrive

We begin slowly. No performance. No pressure. Just presence.

2

Reflect

Archetypal prompts reveal how you move under pressure, desire, and change.

3

Mirror

The group reflects what it sees — using “I experience…” language, not advice.

4

Integrate

You leave with a pattern to watch, and a simple shift to practice.

Why Alchemy Works

Most self-work happens alone. But many of our deepest patterns were formed in relationship — and can only be seen clearly in relationship. Alchemy replaces self-judgment with awareness.

Who This Is For

Alchemy is for people curious about how they change — and why. No prior knowledge of archetypes is needed.
