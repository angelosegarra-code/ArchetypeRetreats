Alchemy for Organizations

A structured way to move from friction to insight — without therapy language.

Alchemy helps teams surface recurring patterns (pressure, desire, avoidance, conflict) and shift how the system responds. It’s not training and it’s not a workshop full of advice — it’s a guided practice where the group learns to observe itself.

What this is

A facilitator-led session using a simple card-based method to help people name what’s happening, choose a different response, and practice it together in real time.

  • Plain and functional: language stays practical — no jargon required.
  • Second-order change: we don’t just “solve the issue,” we shift the pattern that recreates it.
  • Low-risk participation: people speak from observation, not confession.

How it works (60–90 minutes)

1

Choose the pattern

Participants select 2–3 cards that accurately describe what’s happening right now.

2

Name it without blame

The facilitator helps translate complaint into observation so the system can see itself.

3

Shift the response

We practice a new micro-move: a sentence, boundary, request, or reframe the team can repeat.

4

Turn it into a team agreement

We finish with one clear commitment that changes behavior in the next week.

Typical starting modules: Pressure, Desire, Avoidance, Conflict. Teams can book a single session or a short series for deeper integration.
