Alchemy for Organizations

A structured way to move from friction to insight — without therapy language.

Alchemy helps teams surface recurring patterns (pressure, desire, avoidance, conflict) and shift how the system responds. It’s not training — it’s a guided session where the group learns to observe itself.

Team alignment Conflict repair Change readiness Culture + communication Decision clarity

What this is

  • Plain and functional: language stays practical — no jargon required.
  • Second-order change: we don’t just “solve the issue,” we shift the pattern that recreates it.
  • Low-risk participation: people speak from observation, not confession.

How it works (60–90 minutes)

1

Choose the pattern

Participants select 2–3 cards that accurately describe what’s happening right now.

2

Name it without blame

The facilitator helps translate complaint into observation so the system can see itself.

3

Shift the response

We practice a new micro-move: a sentence, boundary, request, or reframe the team can repeat.

4

Turn it into a team agreement

We capture one simple agreement the team can test for 7 days, then review.

Optional follow-up: a second session improves results because the team returns with real data from the week.
