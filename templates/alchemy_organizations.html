What this is

  • A facilitator-led session using a simple, card-based method
  • Names what’s happening now — without blame
  • Shifts from “solving issues” to changing the pattern that recreates them
  • Ends with a clear micro-agreement the team can repeat

Language stays plain and functional. No diagnosis. No labels. No forced vulnerability.

How it works (60–90 minutes)

1

Choose the pattern

Participants select 2–3 cards that accurately describe what’s happening right now.

2

Name it without blame

The facilitator translates complaint into observation so the system can see itself.

3

Shift the response

We practice a new micro-move: a sentence, boundary, request, or reframe the team can repeat.

4

Make it repeatable

The session ends with a clear agreement and a simple “watch-for” pattern to track in real time.

Good fit / Not a fit

  • Good fit: Teams willing to observe patterns, not just debate positions.
  • Good fit: Leaders who want clarity without blame or “therapy talk.”
  • Not a fit: Spaces that require confession, shame, or emotional performance.
  • Not a fit: Teams seeking a quick fix without changing their behaviors.

Common use cases

  • Recurring conflict, looping meetings, or repeated misunderstandings
  • Leadership misalignment and decision paralysis
  • Change readiness and resistance patterns
  • Culture + communication breakdowns after growth or re-org
  • Cross-functional friction (handoffs, accountability, priorities)

FAQ

  • Is this therapy? No. This is structured facilitation focused on team patterns and practical changes.
  • Do people have to share personal details? No. Participation is observation-based, not confession-based.
  • What if the team is defensive? That’s normal. We work with what’s present and keep language clean and safe.
  • Do you provide training materials? You’ll receive a recap and repeatable language. If you want training, we can add it.
