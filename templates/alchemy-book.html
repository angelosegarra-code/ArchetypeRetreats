What This Book Is

The Alchemy of Change is a short, foundational work on second-order change: the kind of transformation that happens when a system becomes aware of itself.

It explores the narrative field — how meaning forms, how patterns persist, and why awareness changes what problem-solving cannot.

This is the moment when the system begins to be aware that it is a system.
