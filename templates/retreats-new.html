The Retreat Experience

This Is Not Just a Vacation

Many people arrive exhausted from years of overthinking, overworking, or feeling disconnected from themselves.

Somewhere along the way, creativity, presence, and meaning became secondary to survival and responsibility.

These retreats are designed to create space — space to slow down, reflect, create, reconnect, and remember who you are beneath the noise.

The Location

Ubud, Bali

Surrounded by rice fields, jungle pathways, cafés, temples, and tropical scenery, Ubud offers a slower rhythm of life designed for reflection, creativity, and renewal. Known as the cultural heart of Bali, the area blends natural beauty with art, spirituality, and warm hospitality in a way that feels both grounding and inspiring.

Accommodation

Boutique Comfort & Shared Gathering Spaces

Guests stay in carefully selected boutique guesthouses, villas, and small hotels throughout the peaceful Ubud area, many within walking distance of cafés, rice fields, and retreat activities. The central gathering space serves as the heart of the retreat experience, offering workshops, shared meals, conversation, and creative reflection in a calm and welcoming atmosphere.

Held by Angelo Segarra

Angelo Segarra is an American expat, artist, writer, and facilitator living in Indonesia. Recently remarried and fully immersed in his own creative renaissance, he brings together decades of experience in art, teaching, philosophy, and cross-cultural living.

His retreats blend painting, journaling, archetypal awareness, and gentle guidance to help participants reconnect with creativity and purpose — no matter their age or stage of life.

The work is quiet, reflective, creative, and direct — designed for people ready to return to themselves through attention, expression, and meaningful change.

Questions You May Have

Do I need artistic experience?

No. These retreats are designed for beginners and experienced creatives alike. The focus is reflection and expression, not performance.

Is this therapy?

No. This is reflective retreat work focused on creativity, self-awareness, and meaningful life integration.

Can I attend alone?

Yes. Many participants arrive alone and quickly feel welcomed into the atmosphere of the retreat.

What ages attend?

Participants come from many different stages of life. The retreat is designed to be inclusive and intergenerational.

Is lodging included?

Yes. Accommodation is included in all immersion options. Room type depends on the option selected: shared villa, private room, or upgraded/private accommodation.
