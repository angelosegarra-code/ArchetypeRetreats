Questions You May Have

Do I need experience with art or creativity?

No. Creative exploration here is not about making “good” art. It is used as a way of seeing what thought alone often misses.

Is this therapy?

No. This is not therapy or medical treatment. It is reflective, experiential retreat work focused on clarity, pattern recognition, and integration.

Can I come alone?

Yes. Most people can attend alone. The experience is designed to be carefully held and supportive.

Is lodging included?

Lodging is not included in the listed individual immersion price, but guidance can be provided depending on retreat location and dates.

What happens after I schedule a call?

We’ll clarify fit, timing, retreat format, and next steps. If it is not the right match, that will be respected.
