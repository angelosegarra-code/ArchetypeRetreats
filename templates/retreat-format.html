Retreats designed for depth, not scale.

Each retreat format is intentionally structured to support clarity, reflection, and meaningful transformation. Rather than offering one-size-fits-all packages, the work is organized around fit, depth, and the kind of container needed for genuine change.

Some people benefit most from an intimate group setting. Others need a more private or tailored format. The options below are designed to make that distinction clear.

Available formats

Bali Immersion

A small-group retreat for individuals seeking clarity, archetypal orientation, and a deeper shift in how they relate to life.

Designed for those who benefit from shared inquiry, spacious setting, and collective depth.

Private Retreat

A one-to-one or paired retreat format for those who want a more focused, personalized, and quiet container.

Best for people moving through a major transition, creative redirection, or a period that calls for concentrated attention.

Group & Teams

A custom retreat structure for partners, founders, leadership groups, or curated teams seeking deeper collective clarity.

This format supports communication, hidden pattern recognition, and more intentional group alignment.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need prior experience with archetypes or retreat work?

No. The work is guided in a way that does not require prior knowledge.

Is accommodation included?

Depending on the retreat format, accommodation may be included or handled separately. Details are clarified before final booking.

Is this therapy?

No. This is not therapy. It is a reflective, facilitated retreat process designed for awareness, orientation, and deeper change.

How do I know which format is right for me?

The discovery call is the best place to begin. It helps clarify fit, readiness, and which format makes the most sense.

Can private retreats be customized?

Yes. Private formats are designed with flexibility in mind and may be shaped around your intention and context.

What happens after I apply or schedule a call?

You will receive next steps, availability, and guidance on the most aligned retreat option.
