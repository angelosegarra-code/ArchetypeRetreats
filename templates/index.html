The Work

Four ways of seeing. One path of becoming.

The archetypes are not labels. They are mirrors — ways of recognizing how imagination, awareness, transformation, and vision move through you.

Imagination, longing, possibility, and the part of you that still believes something new can emerge.

Awareness, clarity, discernment, and the ability to see the pattern beneath the pattern.

Alchemist

Transformation, release, resilience, and the capacity to turn experience into wisdom.

Visionary

Direction, purpose, intuition, and the courage to move toward what wants to take form.
