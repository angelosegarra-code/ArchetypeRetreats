Are you exhausted… no matter how much you rest?

Maybe it shows up as brain fog, low energy, anxiety, or the constant feeling that you are running on empty.

You keep pushing through your days — doing what needs to be done, handling responsibilities, taking care of everyone else.

But deep down, you know something isn’t right.

Living in a constant state of pressure takes a real toll on your body, mind, and spirit. Over time, it can leave you feeling depleted, disconnected, and wondering where the clear, energized version of you went.

Why Rest Isn’t Working

Most people assume burnout is solved with rest. Take time off. Sleep more. Step away.

And for a moment, it helps.

But then something familiar returns: the same thoughts, the same reactions, the same pressure.

Because what you are experiencing isn’t just exhaustion.

It is a pattern.

A way of thinking, responding, and moving through life that has been running for a long time.

And when that pattern does not change, no amount of rest fully resets you.

Real change begins when you can actually see the structure beneath what you have been experiencing.

That is what this retreat is designed to help you do.

This Is Not an Escape. It Is a Recalibration.

Archetype Retreats are designed to give you the space, support, and time to step out of the noise and see what has been shaping your life.

Through guided reflection, archetypal inquiry, creative exploration, nourishing environments, and carefully held dialogue, you begin to reconnect with yourself in a way that feels grounded and real.

You do not come here to be fixed.

You come here to become clear.

By the Time You Leave

You won’t just feel rested.

Who This Is For

This retreat is for men who have been carrying responsibility for a long time and know they need more than a temporary break.

It is for men who feel tired, unclear, disconnected, or caught in a pattern they can no longer ignore.

It is for men who are ready to slow down, listen deeply, and return to life with more honesty, calm, and direction.

Who This Is Not For

This is not a luxury escape, party retreat, or motivational event.

It is not for people looking for a quick fix, passive entertainment, or surface-level inspiration.

This is for people willing to slow down enough to actually see what is shaping their life.

Held by Angelo Segarra

Angelo Segarra is the founder of Archetype Retreats. His work explores creativity, archetypal orientation, second-order change, and the deeper patterns that shape how people experience their lives.

These retreats bring together his background in art, teaching, facilitation, philosophy, and lived experience across cultures.

The work is quiet, reflective, creative, and direct — designed for people who are ready for more than another temporary reset.

Questions You May Have

Do I need experience with art or creativity?

No. Creative exploration here is not about making “good” art. It is used as a way of seeing what thought alone often misses.

Is this therapy?

No. This is not therapy or medical treatment. It is reflective, experiential retreat work focused on clarity, pattern recognition, and integration.

Can I come alone?

Yes. Most people can attend alone. The experience is designed to be carefully held and supportive.

Is lodging included?

Lodging is not included in the listed individual immersion price, but guidance can be provided depending on retreat location and dates.
